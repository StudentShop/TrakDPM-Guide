Start Up Procedure
******************

1.  Rotate the `ON/OFF switch`_ located on the back of the machine to the `ON` position.

.. _`ON/OFF switch`: _images/mainpower.jpg

2.  Flip the `toggle switch`_ located on the right side of the pendant to the `UP position`_.

.. _`toggle switch`: _images/side.jpg
.. _`UP position`: _images/cntlpnlside.jpg

3.  Press the `CHECK SYSTEMS`_ button when the screen turns on. Please note that this is not a touch screen.  Use the soft buttons located on the bottom of the pendant.

.. _`CHECK SYSTEMS`: _images/cksys.jpg

The Trak DPM2 is now ready to run!
